Company Profile

Shanghai Wensi Intelligent Technology Co., Ltd. was established in November 2020 and is located in the Lingang New Area of China (Shanghai) Pilot Free Trade Zone. The core technologies independently developed by the company, including continuous temperature monitoring technology and non-contact vital sign monitoring technology, have achieved important results in multiple fields such as the Internet of Things, artificial intelligence, smart wearables, and intelligent discipline inspection and supervision. Our team has gathered numerous elites from well-known enterprises and top research institutions such as Huawei, Baidu, ZTE, and Tsinghua Flexible Electronics Technology Research Institute. The core members all have rich experience in developing medical grade high-precision and high reliability products. Adhering to a rigorous and meticulous scientific attitude, we are committed to creating high-quality products that satisfy our customers. Looking ahead to the future, the company takes "leading the era of precision in life information" as its corporate vision, and is committed to becoming a leader in the industry and moving towards the goal of becoming a world-class enterprise.
